

## Rajeev Jain — AI Agent Hub

A clean, professional website showcasing Rajeev Jain's expertise in AI Agents, serving as a portfolio, educational resource, blog, and training hub.

### Pages & Structure

**Home Page**
- Hero section with Rajeev Jain's name, title (e.g., "AI Agent Expert & Educator"), and a brief tagline
- Featured highlights: cards for latest blog posts, top resources, and upcoming courses
- "What are AI Agents?" brief explainer section with key concepts
- Testimonials or stats section

**Resources Page**
- Categorized resource library: Articles, Guides, Tutorials, Tools, Frameworks
- Each resource card with title, description, category tag, and link/download
- Filter/search by category
- Sections for: Getting Started, Advanced Topics, Use Cases, Tools & Frameworks

**Contact Page**
- Contact form (name, email, subject, message)
- Links to social profiles (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, GitHub)
- Brief bio sidebar with photo placeholder

### Shared Components
- Clean top navigation bar with logo/name + page links
- Footer with social links, copyright, and quick nav
- Responsive design for mobile and desktop

### Design
- Light, professional color palette (white background, dark navy text, subtle blue accents)
- Clean typography, generous whitespace
- Card-based layouts for resources and blog previews
